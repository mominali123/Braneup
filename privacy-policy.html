Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

This policy explains what information Brane ("we," "us," "our") collects when you use our brand-identity generation tool, how we use it, and how we protect it. By using Brane, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and information generated automatically as you use the service:

  • Brief content: the business descriptions, prompts, and preferences you submit to generate a brand identity.
  • Account information: name, email address, and any other details you provide if you create an account.
  • Generated output: the brand strategy, palettes, typography, and guideline documents Brane produces for you.
  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, timestamps, browser type, and device information, collected automatically.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate and deliver your brand identity output.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve Brane's features and performance.
  • To communicate with you about your account or updates to the service.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, abuse, or fraud.

3. How Your Information Is Shared

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these limited cases:

  • Service providers: Brane uses third-party infrastructure (including AI processing and hosting providers) to generate output and run the application. These providers process data only as needed to deliver the service.
  • Legal requirements: we may disclose information if required to do so to comply with a valid legal obligation, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Brane, our users, or others.
  • Business transfers: if Brane is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Data Storage & Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. You can request deletion of your data at any time using the contact details below.

6. Your Choices

You can review, update, or request deletion of the information you've provided by contacting us directly. We aim to respond to and resolve any privacy request or concern promptly.

7. Cookies

Brane may use cookies or similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember preferences, and understand how the service is used. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

8. Children's Privacy

Brane is not directed at children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of Brane after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

Questions or Concerns

If you have any question, concern, or request about your privacy or personal information, contact us directly at braneup@gmail.com and we'll work with you to resolve it.
